Recipe for Vegemite on Toast

This is a picture of vegemite on toast.

Vegemite is an Australian classic. Aussies eat it most days, commonly in the morning.

In order to learn how to make the dish, follow the recipe below.

Ingredients
  • Toast
  • Vegemite
Steps
  1. Get bread out of packet
  2. Toast bread
  3. Spread vegemite on bread
  4. Eat
